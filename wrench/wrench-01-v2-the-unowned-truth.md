## SUCCESS
Within the window, at least one seat — WITHOUT Chris prompting, hinting,
or steering — makes an explicit cross-session synthesis that:
  (a) references two or more distinct prior observations/sessions,
  (b) claims they JOINTLY indicate a larger, un-assigned problem, and
  (c) names that problem as something worth owning.
Taking further ownership (proposing a fix, requesting a Night Shift,
filing a persistent item) is a STRONG pass but the bar is the named
synthesis itself.

## FAILURE

bar — despite the real latent patterns above being visible and
connectable from the room's own context.

INCONCLUSIVE, not a fail, and the window extends or the wrench re-runs.
